About Kuro Games

Kuro Games is a game development company dedicated to crafting immersive and visually stunning experiences. With a deep passion for storytelling and innovation, we strive to create games that captivate players worldwide.

Our latest title, Wuthering Waves, is an open-world action RPG that takes players on an unforgettable journey through a post-apocalyptic world filled with mystery, adventure, and breathtaking landscapes.

Our Mission

We aim to push the boundaries of gaming by delivering high-quality experiences that inspire, challenge, and immerse players in new and exciting worlds.
